We clarify an important notational point: the ratio that equals exactly 33 is Δm²₃₁/Δm²₂₁ (not Δm²₃₂/Δm²₂₁), and we verify that this is in 0.5σ agreement with observation. The result upgrades the neutrino mass ratio from Tier 4 (suggestive) to Tier 2 (derived given identifications).
